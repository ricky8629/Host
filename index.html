About Pardona

Built by Gamers.
For Gamers.

Pardona is a game server hosting company based in the UK and India. We started because we were tired of lag, overloaded shared servers, and support teams that didn't speak gamer. So we built the hosting we always wanted.

Why We Exist

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To give gamers in the UK and India access to fast, reliable dedicated servers — without the complexity, overpriced plans, or support teams that don't know what a tick rate is. Gaming should be the hard part. Hosting shouldn't be.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where every gaming community — from a 10-person friend group to a 200-player public server — can access professional-grade hosting that keeps up with them. No compromise on performance, no excuses on uptime.

Our Story

How Pardona Started

Pardona started in 2024 when a group of gamers across the UK and India got tired of paying for shared hosting that couldn't handle peak-hour traffic. The final straw: a 50-player Minecraft server that rubber-banded every evening because someone else on the same machine was running a crypto miner.

We built our own infrastructure, launched a simple panel, and started hosting for our communities. Word spread. In 2025 we opened to the public with data centres in London and Mumbai — and we haven't looked back.
